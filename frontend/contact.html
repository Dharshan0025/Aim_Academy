Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our academy is open Monday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll either by visiting our campus in person or through our online admission portal. For new students, we recommend scheduling a counseling session first to determine the most suitable course.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers. We also offer installment payment plans for certain courses.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online classes for most of our courses. Our online platform includes live interactive sessions, recorded lectures, and digital study materials.

What safety measures are in place at your campus?

We maintain strict hygiene protocols, regular sanitization, and social distancing measures. All our staff are fully vaccinated, and we encourage all eligible students to get vaccinated as well.
